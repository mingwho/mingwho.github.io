Special Effect Makeup

Using prosthetic sculpting, molding and casting techniques to create advanced cosmetic effects; Uncomfortable and bloody content alert!

In 2017 Spring at NYU, I took Intro to Special Effect Makeup with Robert Benevides at NYU Tisch Open Arts Curriculum. I learned how to mold, cast and paint silicone replica props; how to apply make-up effects such as cuts, bruises, scars, wounds, and burned flesh; and how to design and execute a zombie make up and how to sculpt a 1:1 animal character maquette.
